Kylie Jenner Shows Off Fabulous Curves While Posing Next To Her Mercedes-Benz SUV

 Posted on February 25, 2024  |  2 minutes  |  366 words  |  Martina Birk

Let’s not forget that Kylie Jenner is a billionaire and if that tidbit was going to slip your mind, Kylie’s toys will remind you. For instance, Kylie has a lot of cars, including a gold-colored Mercedes Benz SUV and she posed by it wearing a curve-hugging black and white catsuit that showed off her fabulous curves. Sharing several photos on her Instagram account, where she now has a whopping 158.5 million followers, the pictures went viral as fans got a close look at her luxury vehicle. Kylie also shared a photo from inside the vehicle where people praised her manicure, even Paris Hilton sounded off!

Kylie has been busy getting her Stormi Collection ready for its February 1, 2020, launch —the same day that her daughter celebrates her second birthday. The Stormi Collection features 11 pieces and the makeup consists of metallic and sparkling colors in shades of purple, pink, and gold. Over the weekend, Kylie shared multiple photos and videos of herself posing with Stormi for the campaign.

In the photos below, Kylie is posing by her vehicle with her leg resting against the SUV’s edge as if she is getting ready to climb inside. Wearing a black and white patterned catsuit, she paired the outfit with a gray purse that also featured an intricate design. She wore black and red Nike sneakers.

Kylie’s makeup was done with light pink shades on her eyes and a dark-nude, matte color on her lips. Kylie has been experimenting with different length hair and wore her hair straight and falling just below her shoulders.
