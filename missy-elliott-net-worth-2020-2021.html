Early Life

Melissa Arnette Elliott, popularly known as Missy “Misdemeanor” Elliott, was born on July 1, 1971, in Portsmouth, Virginia, to her parents Ronnie Elliott and Patricia Elliott. As a child, she used to perform in the church choir.

Career

In 2011, she released the album ‘Miss E… So Addictive.’ It was followed by the album ‘Under Construction.’ She then went on to release the album ‘This Is Not a Test!’ in 2003. Missy Elliott has also released the compilation albums ‘Recipe of Hits’, ‘Respect M.E.’, and ‘Original Album Series.’ She has released three box sets, 20 music videos, and 74 singles.
