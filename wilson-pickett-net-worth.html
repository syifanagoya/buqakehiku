Wilson Pickett, an iconic figure in soul and R&B music, had a career that spanned decades, marked by numerous hits and significant contributions to the genre.

Career Beginnings

Pickett started his musical journey with gospel group The Violinaires and later joined The Falcons. His early involvement in these groups laid the foundation for his successful solo career.

Signing with Atlantic Records was a turning point. Hits like In the Midnight Hour and Mustang Sally catapulted him to fame, solidifying his status as one of the most successful singers of his time.
